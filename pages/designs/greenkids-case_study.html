Problem Statement: Parents and guardians wants a way to buy clothes for their childrens and resell their old ones easily.
Solution: Design an app for the users to buy and resell children's clothes.

Secondary Research
  • The Indian kids‚Äô apparel market is valued at ~USD 22 billion (2024), growing steadily with rising income, urbanization, and e-commerce.
  • Urban parents increasingly seek branded yet affordable clothing for their children.
  • Secondhand clothing is gaining popliarity post-COVID, as families look for sustainable and cost-saving options.

Hypothesized Pain Points
  • Children clothes outgrow quickly, making well-conditioned clothes unusable in a short time.
  • Accumulation of unused clothes create clutter at home, making it difficult to manage space effectively.
  • Parents struggle to find easy and trustworthy platform to resell chilren‚Äôs old clothes.

Competitive audit and report
I have done the competitive audit of four companies and made a report.
Key Findings
  • Hopscotch: Variety of clothes but no resell option.
  • The Share Bear: Great UI but website available only in English.
  • eBay: Sellers can open stores and promote listings but overwhelming for the first users.
  • OLX: Direct chat between buyer and seller but too much promotional content on some pages.
